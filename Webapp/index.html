Welcome To The Maze Runners Online App

Welcome to the webapp of The Maze Runners program. The Maze Runners program is an C++11 multiplayer maze game, based on the Maze C program.

When the gameserver has been started, a live overview of the gamemap can be seen on the Live Gamemap page. Make sure that the GameServer and the Demons programm are started before you can start player(s) and play the game.
